You want the **most common, high-frequency Chinese characters and words** for daily survival—the ones that actually appear in 80% of everyday conversations. No fluff, no rare literary characters. 

I’ve organized this into **tiers of importance** and **real-life scenarios**. Master these, and you can survive anything from ordering food to asking for directions to making small talk.
